Detailed Description

Device client.

Represents connection between an app and device. Typically an ap has at most one client, but this is not a requirement.

Devices asks RequestHandler to create Client object when a new client comes to the devices, and asks to remove it when the client leaves.

You may subclass Client if you need to keep additional per-client state. To incorporate your owne client class, you'll need to set custom RequestHandler.
