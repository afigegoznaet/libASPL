◆ EnableMixing

bool aspl::DeviceParameters::EnableMixing = true

Whether IORequestHandler will handle mixed output from all clients, or handle individual clients and perform mixing by itself.

  • When true, Device will invoke IORequestHandler::OnProcessMixedOutput() and IORequestHandler::WriteMixedOutput(). It will be provided with the result of mixing of all clients.
  • When false, Device will invoke IORequestHandler::OnProcessClientOutput() and IORequestHandler::WriteClientOutput(). It will be repeatedly called with the output from each client. IORequestHandler is resposible for mixing in this case.
